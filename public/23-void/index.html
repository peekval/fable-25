01 — Practice

We do not add. We take away, until the wall, the light and the hour agree.

A room is finished when nothing can be removed without the silence collapsing. We build that moment, and then we leave.

Three materials. One drawing. No renderings — the sun does not read PDFs.

02 — Light Study

study n° 4 — one wall, twelve hours

The sun is our only client with taste.

Before any wall is poured, we watch its light for a full day. If the beam finds nothing worth touching, the wall is redrawn. This one passed on the fourth attempt.
