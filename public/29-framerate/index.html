Program

Reel 02 · Four world premieres

01

Sprocket Hymn

Ilse Marchetti · 16mm · 41 min

A choir of retired projectionists loops the same eighteen frames until the emulsion learns to sing. Shot entirely inside a lamphouse.

02

Magnetic North / True North

Ossian Berg · Super 8 · 63 min

Two compasses argue across a split screen while the Arctic melts between them. The soundtrack is the ice, slowed 24 times.

03

The Persistence of Vision

Q. Adeyemi · 35mm · 78 min

Twenty-four lies per second, told slowly enough to become documentary. A love letter to every frame the eye refused to see.

04

Room Tone

Vera Lindqvist · Field recording & light · 60 min

Sixty minutes of silence recorded in demolished cinemas. Projected onto haze, the walls finally answer back.

Venues

Reel 03 · Three rooms, one beam

Lichtspiel Palast

Main house · 600 seats

The last carbon-arc lamphouse in the country, restored frame by frame. 35mm and 70mm change-over projection, red velvet, no trailers.

Die Kapelle

Expanded cinema · 120 standing

A deconsecrated chapel for multi-projector performances and 360° loops. Bring a coat; the light is the only heating.

Silo 9

Harbour silo · vertical screen

A grain silo turned 30-metre vertical screen for the closing 24-hour marathon. Films begin at sunset and end when the stock runs out.
