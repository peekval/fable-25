01 — THE STUDIO

Every world starts
as one cube.

VOXELWERK was founded in a Hamburg attic by two level designers who kept prototyping "one last cozy idea" after work. We ship games the way we build dioramas: block out the feeling first, then place every cube on purpose. Small scope, deep polish, zero filler quests.

OUT NOW

Emberwick

COZY FORGE-KEEPING · PC / SWITCH

You inherit the last warm forge in a town that forgot how to make things. Reignite it, one commission at a time — hammer voxel blades for nervous knights, kettles for grandmothers, and courage for yourself. 94% positive, and the anvil sound is famous now.

2027 — WISHLIST

Mossdeep

UNDERWATER PILGRIMAGE · PC

A slow dive through a drowned valley where every ruin was somebody's home. Guide a lantern-fish through kelp cathedrals, restore the reef cube by cube, and learn why the water rose. No fail state. No timer. Just depth.

Wanderlicht

FLOATING-ISLAND HOMESTEAD · PC / SWITCH · COMING 2027

The third world, painted before it's built. You tend a wandering island that ferries lost travellers home — plant windbreaks, keep the hearth lit, and follow the fireflies when the map runs out. This key art is the mood target; every cube of it gets rebuilt in-engine, block by stubborn block.

03 — THE WERKSHOP

How a world
gets werked.

S1

Blockout the feeling

Grey cubes, no art. If the island isn't charming as raw geometry, no texture will save it.

S2

Swap the palette

Same island, new mood. The dusk build you see here is Emberwick's terrain wearing Mossdeep's colors.

S3

Play it for a week

Every cube earns its place or gets deleted. Our repo history is mostly removals.
