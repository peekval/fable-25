feed 03 — doctrine

03 / What we believe

THE COLLECTIVE MANIFESTO

宣言 — written on a napkin in 2077. Still binding.

街は我々から夜を奪った。だから我々は火と光で夜を取り戻す。 The city took the night from us — so we took it back with fire and light. A bowl of noodles at 3 a.m. is not commerce. It is resistance you can slurp.

No franchises直火主義

Every stall is owner-burned. If the person who invented the recipe isn't sweating over it tonight, it isn't served tonight.

Feed the blackout停電上等

When the grid fails, we don't close — we get brighter. Generators first, profits later. Nobody eats cold in Sector 7.

Price like it's 2077据え置き

Inflation is a corporate weather system. Our menu prices haven't moved in nine years and never will.

Leave no one hungry残さず食え

Last hour of every night: pay what you carry. Even if what you carry is nothing but a good story.
