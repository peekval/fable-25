Look 01 — Opening exit

Coat, Removed

A double-breasted greatcoat, drafted at full scale and never sewn. The shoulder line is drawn by whatever refuses to fill it; the lapel breaks over air.

Look 02 — Eveningwear

The Bias Argument

Charmeuse cut on the true bias falls at forty-five degrees or not at all. Here the drape is the thesis and the body a footnote, politely declined.

Note from the atelier

We removed the photographs the way one removes a lining — to see what the seam was doing all along. A garment is mostly air held in place; the cloth is only there to persuade the air to stay. This season we kept the persuasion and released the cloth.

— The cutting room, Rue de Braque, Paris IIIᵉ
