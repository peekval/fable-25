02 Voice Architecture

Signal path, exactly

A / B / C

VCO Bank

Three temperature-compensated discrete oscillators share one exponential converter. Spread them apart with the DETUNE pot and the beat frequencies do what a chorus pedal only pretends to.

−24 dB/OCT

Ladder VCF

A four-pole transistor ladder, matched by hand from batches of two hundred. Push RES past three o'clock and it sings on its own — that's a feature, and it always was.

CLASS A

VCA & Output

A single Class-A output stage, no soft-clip magic, no DSP apology. What leaves the 6.3 mm jack is the ladder's opinion, amplified and nothing else.

// Calibration note — unit 07-023

Each OSC-1 leaves the bench only after a 48-hour burn-in and a manual scale trim across five octaves. The trimmers are then sealed with a drop of green lacquer.

If your unit drifts more than two cents within the warranty period, send it back. We retune it, re-seal it, and add a second stamp below the first. Some units in circulation carry four.

— F. Keller, final test
