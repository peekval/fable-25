How this mess was made

The techniques

Every texture on RIOT PAPER is CSS — there is not a single image. The torn-paper cards are many-point polygon clip-paths on a ::before layer, never on the card itself, while filter:drop-shadow sits on the parent: that ordering trick makes the shadow follow the ragged edge and lets tape strips and staples overhang the tear instead of being clipped. The masking tape is a translucent clipped strip with faint repeating stripes, the staples are tiny inline SVGs, and the "APPROVED BY NO ONE" rubber stamps get their uneven ink from a radial mask-image, slamming in once at 3x scale when they scroll into view. A fixed pseudo-layer of two offset dot grids with mix-blend-mode:multiply reads as photocopy toner over the whole page, with a toner smear down the right edge. Cards tilt a couple of degrees each way and jitter on hover with a steps(2) animation, so they skip like a dying Xerox.
