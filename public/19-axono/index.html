Approach

Four passes,
one drawing.

01 / SURVEY

Read the ground

Before a single line: sun path, noise contours, pedestrian desire lines, the datum of every neighbouring eave. The site sets the rules; we transcribe them.

02 / MASSING

Volume before facade

We test the block at 1:500 until the shadows behave — stepping heights to keep courtyards lit past 15:00 in December, holding street walls at 21 metres.

03 / JUNCTION

Draw every junction

Parapet to soffit, sill to slab edge: each junction is detailed at 1:5 before tender. A building fails at its joints, never at its renders.

04 / SITE

Build what was drawn

We stay on site weekly through the shell. Tolerances are agreed in millimetres, deviations logged against the model — the drawing remains the contract.
