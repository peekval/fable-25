MAESTRO — Conduct

How this experience works — and the prompt that rebuilds it. All of it is code: no image or audio assets, everything procedural.

01 A synthesized orchestra

Strings (detuned saw ensemble through slow-attack lowpass), winds (filtered triangles with vibrato), brass, timpani (tuned noise + membrane drop) and celesta bells — all pure Web Audio, summed through a feedback-delay reverb and a gentle compressor. Harmony is generative but always consonant: a slow minor progression advancing on a clock, instruments playing humanized chord tones.

02 Scroll conducts

Audio begins only after an explicit gesture. Then the page is the score: sections add and remove instrument groups, scroll velocity is dynamics (limited so it never blares), cursor height shapes expression. Standing still holds a breathing chord.

03 Sound made visible

Every visual voice — bow-lines, breath curls, timpani rings — is driven by live AnalyserNode data, so image and sound are one. With audio off, a simulation drives the same visuals; the page is complete in silence.

04 The coda

'Your performance': duration, peak dynamic, bars conducted — and the streams take a bow.

Reproduction prompt
Build a single-file generative-music experience called "MAESTRO — Conduct." Velvet black (#0D0B07), gold (#C9A25E), a dignified serif + mono. Synthesize a full orchestra in Web Audio (no audio files): strings as a detuned sawtooth ensemble through a slow-attack lowpass, winds as filtered triangles with LFO vibrato, brass with harder envelopes, timpani from tuned noise bursts plus a sine membrane drop, celesta as short bells; master chain = feedback-delay-network reverb + gentle compressor, modest gain. Harmony: a generative, always-consonant system — slow minor progression (i-VI-III-VII) on a clock, instruments choosing humanized chord tones/arpeggios. NO sound before an explicit "Begin" click (construct the AudioContext lazily); always-visible mute toggle. Conducting: scrolling through movements adds/removes instrument groups (strings -> winds -> tutti -> decrescendo to solo), scroll velocity maps to dynamics through a limiter, cursor height controls vibrato/brightness, stillness holds a soft sustained chord. Visuals on canvas, driven by live AnalyserNode data (or a simulation when muted): long glowing bow-lines per string voice, breath curls for winds, expanding rings on timpani hits; at the end, "Your performance" (duration, peak dynamic, bars) and the streams dip in a bow. Suspend the AudioContext on hidden tabs; prefers-reduced-motion calms visuals but leaves audio available; no horizontal overflow at 390px; zero console errors with and without Begin.
