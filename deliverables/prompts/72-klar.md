# KLAR — Marketing ohne Lärm (Site 72)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for KLAR, a fictional Swiss marketing agency in Zürich whose positioning is "Marketing ohne Lärm" — all copy in de-CH German (no ß: gross, heisst). It must read as the cleanest site imaginable: off-white #FAF8F4 paper, near-black ink #171614, exactly one accent coral #FF5A48, hairline rules at 14% ink, whitespace treated as a statement. Typography: Google's Archivo variable font only, exploiting the width axis — wordmark and display lines at wdth 116–125, weight 800, −0.03em tracking; body at normal width; micro-labels uppercase at 0.72rem with 0.28–0.34em letterspacing. Motion discipline is the thesis: declare ONE easing, cubic-bezier(.3,.7,0,1), use it for every transition, keep durations 0.35–0.5s, and allow exactly one reveal move (14px rise plus fade, fired once via IntersectionObserver). Signature: a coral dot that is literally the full stop of the huge KLAR wordmark; on scroll it detaches (smoothstep blend, 0.16 lerp inside a single requestAnimationFrame loop) and rides a fixed 1px hairline rail at the left margin, its y-position mapping total scroll progress — the agency's belief in reduction made kinetic. Sections: (1) near-empty 100svh hero — kicker, wordmark with the dot, one sentence: "Wir machen aus Botschaften Ansagen." (2) Arbeiten: four fictional cases as full-width hairline-separated rows (Klient huge and uppercase, one-line Aufgabe, right-aligned coral result number counting up on first view with the same cubic ease-out: +212% Markenerinnerung, −38% Streuverlust, 4.6× Umsatz, 9 Wörter); hovering a row inverts it flat to coral, gated behind (hover:hover); above the rows a full-bleed night billboard photo sits in a clipped figure with 120% image height and ±7% translateY parallax. (3) Ansatz: a 340vh sticky section showing three principles one at a time, set giant — "Weglassen ist eine Entscheidung." / "Eine Botschaft. Nicht zwei." / "Laut ist einfach. Klar ist Arbeit." — only the active one opaque, no overlap, with a 1/3 counter. (4) Studio: studio photo beside a short team statement and seven monogram circles instead of portraits. (5) Kontakt: one giant mailto line, hallo@klar.studio, then a hairline footer. Under prefers-reduced-motion never start the rAF loop: hide the travelling dot and paint the wordmark's full stop coral in place, show all principles stacked, print final numbers immediately. All images lazy with real width/height; no horizontal overflow at 390px. Generated assets: two 1920×1080 photographs from Nano Banana 2 (Higgsfield MCP) — a night billboard carrying a coral campaign above an empty street, and a bright studio wall of pinned campaign mockups — converted to WebP with ffmpeg.
