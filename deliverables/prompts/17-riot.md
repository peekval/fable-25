# RIOT PAPER — Punk Zine Collage (Site 17)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for RIOT PAPER, a DIY punk zine collective presenting Issue #13: NOISE. Use zero images — every texture must be CSS. Palette: aged paper #F1EFE9, ink #101010, safety-pin red #E8281E, highlighter yellow #F5E642, translucent masking tape rgba(228,220,180,.55). Fonts: Anton for shouted headlines, Permanent Marker for scrawled annotations, Courier Prime for typewriter body copy. Signature technique — the paper-collage system: article cards are torn scraps built with a many-point polygon clip-path applied to a ::before layer (never the card itself) while filter:drop-shadow sits on the parent, so the shadow follows the ragged edge and tape strips can overhang the tear. Masking tape is a clipped translucent strip with faint repeating stripes; staples are tiny inline SVGs; rubber stamps reading "APPROVED BY NO ONE" use a red double border plus a radial mask-image for uneven ink coverage, and slam in once from 3x scale via IntersectionObserver. Lay a fixed photocopy-grain pseudo-layer (two offset radial-gradient dot grids, mix-blend-mode:multiply, ~.18 opacity) over everything, plus a toner smear down the right edge. Cards tilt ±2.4deg and jitter on hover with a steps(2) "dying Xerox" animation. Sections: cover (rotated masthead on black, giant NOISE headline with torn yellow highlight, taped scrap notes), yellow ticker strip, three-article wall on a ruled background with a $0 price sticker, black torn-edge manifesto with a numbered creed and highlighter smears, submit/footer with a taped mail card and a CSS barcode. Copy: sarcastic, self-deprecating punk humour. Reduced motion: kill jitter, ticker and slams; reveals become opacity-only.
