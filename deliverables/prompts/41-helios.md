# HELIOS — Concentrated Solar, Delivered as Heat (FABLE/25 · 41)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file showcase website (index.html, no libraries) for HELIOS — a fictional solar-thermal startup: "Concentrated Solar, Delivered as Heat." Aesthetic: futuristic-clean and LIGHT — generous white space, engineering precision, warmth from one gold accent. Palette: paper #F7F8F6, ink #16181A, sun-gold #FFB627, warm shadow #E8E2D4, dusk blue #1A3A5C. Google Fonts: Archivo (variable width axis ~122–125, uppercase display, weight ~640), Instrument Sans (body), Fragment Mono (uppercase micro-labels, tabular counters).

Signature technique — the sun drives the page. Derive p = scrollY/maxScroll (a full day, dawn→noon→dusk) and elev = sin(pπ). One rAF loop eases p and writes CSS custom properties on :root: (1) ambient light temperature --tint/--sky interpolated through cool dawn #E3EAF0 → white noon #F7F8F6 → golden dusk #F5E2BE, applied to body background and panel tints; (2) ONE global shadow vector --sdx/--sdy/--slen plus --shadow-c that every card, pill and panel uses, so shadows point away from the sun, lengthen at low elevation and warm from grey-brown to amber; (3) a fixed radial-gradient sun disc translated along a viewport arc (low-left → high-center → low-right), core color whitening at noon; (4) a fixed telemetry HUD pill: solar time 05:30–19:30, elevation in degrees, live output "N MWt" = 210·elev^1.15.

Add a canvas heliostat diagram in a bordered panel ("FIELD/01 — heliostat array, top-down"): ~450 mirror strokes on flattened concentric ellipses around a central tower. Each mirror's normal bisects sun-direction and tower-direction; draw the stroke perpendicular to the normal, ease angles by 9%/frame (shortest arc) for a satisfying sweep; add gold catch-lights, a few sampled sun→mirror→tower rays and a receiver glow that scale with elevation. Pause the loop when the tab is hidden or the canvas is offscreen; idle when settled; cap devicePixelRatio at 2.

Sections: hero (huge uppercase headline "CONCENTRATED SOLAR, DELIVERED AS HEAT." with SOLAR in dusk blue and a gold underline scaling with elevation; scroll cue "scroll = one solar day" with a tiny arc); the canvas panel; How it works (3 cards with animated inline SVGs: mirrors track → one focal point → storage dispatches); The Atacama One field — full-bleed assets/field.webp (1920×1080, lazy, real dimensions) revealed by a thin gold sweep line chased by a clip-path inset transition, with mono caption; Numbers (count-up stats: 1050 °C, 16 h, 94 %, 12 €/MWh); Projects ledger (4 rows, coordinates, status dots); dusk-blue CTA footer "Put the sun on your process line." with gold pill button and links.
