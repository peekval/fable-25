# FLORA/FAUNA — Botanical Apothecary (Site 10)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for FLORA/FAUNA, a fictional small-batch botanical skincare apothecary. Palette: sage #A8B5A0, warm cream #F7F3EC, terracotta #C4704F, deep moss #3E4A3D, with pale supports #D8DFD2 and #EFE8DC. Fonts (Google): Fraunces variable — exploit the SOFT, WONK and optical-size axes so display type feels hand-set and slightly wonky — paired with Karla for UI and body. Signature technique: SVG product bottles with living liquid — each of three vials clips two horizontally repeating sine-wave paths inside the bottle silhouette via clipPath, translating them in opposite directions on infinite loops so the liquid sloshes, with tiny bubbles rising on staggered delays; on card hover the whole bottle tips a few degrees from a transform-origin at its base, like real glass being nudged. Supporting motion: huge soft background blobs whose border-radius morphs through keyframes while rotating slowly; hand-drawn botanical sprig line illustrations that draw themselves on load via stroke-dasharray with per-path delays; and a springy cubic-bezier(.34,1.65,.44,1) on every hover lift so cards bounce gently rather than glide. Sections: full-height hero with blobs, sprigs and two pill CTAs ("Grown slow. Bottled small."); a moss-green ingredient marquee (clone the sequence so the −50% loop is seamless); three ritual cards with line icons; a shelf of exactly three products with CHF prices; a dark moss ingredient index with Latin names, one-word uses and leaf icons that spring on row hover; three field-note journal cards; moss footer. Copy tone: slow, honest, sensory craft language — no hype, no synthetics. Freeze every animation under prefers-reduced-motion. Generated asset: one top-down botanical flat-lay photograph made with Nano Banana 2 (Higgsfield MCP, text-to-image, 2K) from a palette-locked prompt — sage leaves, chamomile blossoms, calendula petals and rosemary sprigs on #F7F3EC warm cream linen, one #C4704F terracotta dish of pale-green (#A8B5A0) balm, soft directional daylight — converted to WebP via ffmpeg and placed as a full-width "Gathered / what goes in the bottle" interstitial between the shelf and the ingredient index, framed in the site's 36px-radius hairline card style with a dashed-rule caption: uppercase terracotta batch label on the left, a Fraunces italic line on the right (`loading="lazy" decoding="async"` with real width/height). It is the page's only photograph — everything else stays vector.
