## Full Reproduction Prompt

Bottled small."); a moss-green ingredient marquee (clone the sequence so the −50% loop is seamless); three ritual cards with line icons; a shelf of exactly three products with CHF prices; a dark moss ingredient index with Latin names, one-word uses and leaf icons that spring on row hover; three field-note journal cards; moss footer.
