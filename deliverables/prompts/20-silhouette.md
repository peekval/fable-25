# SILHOUETTE — ABSENCE, AW26 Lookbook (Site 20)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for SILHOUETTE, a Paris fashion house presenting "ABSENCE", Autumn/Winter 2026 — a lookbook with no photographs. Palette: bone #EDEAE4, carbon #161616, one rust accent #B04A2F, hairline rules at 22% opacity in both directions. Fonts: Bodoni Moda (italic, high-contrast serif) for display, Archivo Narrow for small tracked-out uppercase labels. Signature technique — the photography-free runway: a full-bleed horizontal strip on carbon holding eight "look" panels, each dominated by a giant Bodoni numeral (min(20vw,300px)) rendered as transparent fill with a 1px -webkit-text-stroke outline; on hover it skews (-11deg), stretches vertically and floods rust. Even-numbered looks flip the composition — numeral set italic on the right edge, silhouette moved left. Garments are thin-stroke SVG line drawings, abstract dress silhouettes that sway ±1.8deg on slow alternating loops with staggered delays. On desktop, hijack the vertical wheel into horizontal glide (only while the strip isn't at either end), add drag-to-scroll with a grab cursor, scroll-snap proximity, and a live "01 / 08" counter; below 820px the runway restacks vertically. Fabric swatches are small CSS-gradient chips overlaid with an inline fractal-noise SVG filter for cloth grain; the same noise sits at 4.5% opacity over the whole runway. Open with a theatre curtain: two carbon panels part along a rust seam after an italic letterspaced wordmark fades. Sections: bone hero with giant italic "AB–SENCE" and a vertical season tag, serif marquee, the runway (look 04 entirely rust), an atelier note in large serif, credits footer. Copy voice: wry haute-couture editorial — "seating by absence only". Reduced motion: no curtain, static marquee, no sway or hover skew.
