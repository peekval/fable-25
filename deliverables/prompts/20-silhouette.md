# SILHOUETTE — ABSENCE, AW26 Lookbook (Site 20)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for SILHOUETTE, a Paris fashion house presenting "ABSENCE", Autumn/Winter 2026 — a lookbook with no photographs. Palette: bone #EDEAE4, carbon #161616, one rust accent #B04A2F, hairline rules at 22% opacity in both directions. Fonts: Bodoni Moda (italic, high-contrast serif) for display, Archivo Narrow for small tracked-out uppercase labels. Centerpiece — the living cloth: a tall carbon silk banner rendered with three.js (r147 from cdnjs), a high-resolution plane hung in the hero and displaced in the vertex shader by layered curl-noise wind — a simplex potential differentiated into a flow field that advects three octaves of folds — pinned at the top rail, free at a frayed, swinging hem. Shade it like silk: wrap diffuse, crease shadow from displacement depth, anisotropic sheen banding across the folds, bone rim light. The collection word AB–SENCE (SENCE in rust) is drawn to an offscreen canvas in Bodoni italic and texture-projected onto the cloth, so the typography warps and shades with every fold; the DOM headline stays for fallback and screen readers. The cursor is a hand brushing the fabric: a gaussian push at the pointer plus up to six slow travelling ripple rings spawned on press and fast strokes; same from touch. A second, quieter fabric moment: a translucent gauze band drifting behind the atelier note, alpha densest where the weave folds. Keep the photography-free runway: eight look panels on carbon, giant stroked Bodoni numerals flooding rust on hover, thin-stroke SVG garment silhouettes swaying ±1.8°, wheel-hijacked horizontal glide with drag-to-scroll and a live 01/08 counter, restacking vertically below 820px; fabric swatches as CSS gradients under an inline fractal-noise filter; theatre-curtain intro parting on a rust seam. Discipline: DPR capped at 2, rAF paused offscreen (IntersectionObserver) or when the tab hides, lower plane resolution at ≤768px; no WebGL falls back to the static hero; prefers-reduced-motion gets one static draped frame and no loops. Copy voice: wry haute-couture editorial — "seating by absence only".
