# MERCVRY — Atelier for Additive Metalwork (Site 16)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML showcase site for MERCVRY, a Zürich atelier that 3D-prints jewelry and sculpture in sterling silver, titanium and steel. Palette: near-black ink #101114 and #16181d panels, steel greys #DDE3EA / #9AA2AE / #6E7683, one electric accent "volt" #4D5BFF. Fonts: Syne (700/800) for enormous tight headlines, Space Mono for everything else. Signature technique #1 — a ferrofluid hero: a full-viewport canvas renders 2D metaballs (sum of r²/distance² fields sampled at quarter resolution into an offscreen buffer, then upscaled). Where the field exceeds 1, map the pixel to a vertical chrome palette of alternating light/dark steel bands; brighten the rim just above threshold toward blue-white, darken deep cores, and give the anti-aliased skirt a #4D5BFF halo. Five blobs drift on sine paths; a sixth eases toward the cursor (autonomous drift on touch). Signature technique #2 — chrome headlines: a multi-stop silver gradient with background-clip:text whose background-position glides after cursor X (or scroll progress on touch), inside an @supports guard with a solid-silver fallback. Add magnetic buttons with a springy release, a marquee that blurs with scroll velocity, and slow SMIL-animated gradients inside the specimen SVGs. Sections: hero with coordinates HUD, marquee, 01 Process (four brushed-metal panel steps), 02 Specimens (three pieces as SVG drawings with spec plates: alloy, mass, edition, CHF price), 03 Material (copy plus an alloy data table), 04 Commissions, terse footer. Copy voice: precise, poetic industrial luxury — "liquid metal, frozen mid-thought". Pause the rAF loop when the hero is off-screen or the tab is hidden; under prefers-reduced-motion render one static frame and strip the SMIL nodes.
