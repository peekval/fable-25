# 43-aviary — reproduction prompt

Build a single-file showcase website (index.html, no build step, no JS libraries) for AVIARY — "The sky, scheduled." — a fictional autonomous drone-delivery network. Aesthetic: futuristic-clean light-sky with cartographic precision. Palette: sky paper #EAF2F8, cloud white #FFFFFF, ink #1B2733, signal orange #FF6B2C, route blue #3D7DC8, dim #7C8FA3. Fonts via Google Fonts: Bricolage Grotesque for display/body, IBM Plex Mono for all ops data.

Showpiece — a living dispatch map. A fixed full-viewport 2D canvas draws an abstract city in thin cartographic lines: seeded-random irregular street grid, white blocks with faint building-footprint rectangles, a sinusoidal pale-blue river with flow lines, a park blob with tree dots, low-opacity mono labels (SECTOR 01…, RIVER MERN, ROOSEVELT PARK) and circular hub markers. Render everything in world coordinates (about 1600x1000) through one setTransform camera so it can pan and zoom; keep strokes hairline by dividing lineWidth by the scale. On it, 3-5 concurrent delivery flights loop forever: orange origin ping, dashed blue quadratic-bezier route drawing in, a tiny quad-rotor drone glyph flying it with easing and a banking squash on curves, an orange trail, delivery ping, fade out. A mono dispatch feed in the hero corner logs pickups and deliveries. Hero headline sits over the map ("The sky, scheduled." with "scheduled." in orange) behind a soft radial paper scrim.

Then make scroll tell one package's story: a 520vh section with a sticky viewport where scroll progress scrubs a single arc-length-parameterised multi-bezier route from Hub A to a rooftop pad. Camera lerps along the route with keyframed zoom (2.3 at pickup, wider in cruise, tight at delivery); ambient flights cross-fade out. Four checkpoint copy cards fade in at fixed route fractions: pickup at Hub A, Corridor 7 cruise, a wind-advisory reroute where the original path shows as a crossed-out dashed ghost with animated wind chevrons over the river, and rooftop delivery. Add a mono telemetry HUD (ALT/SPD/DIST/status) updating throughout, plus progress dots.

After the journey: an aircraft section presenting assets/drone.webp (1920x1080, lazy, real dims) with SVG callout lines that draw to the rotors, cargo pod and sensor mast (legend list under 860px), and a 4-cell spec strip (18 km, 4.2 kg, 14 m/s, 62 dB). Then count-up network stats, and a zones + waitlist footer with mono zone chips (LIVE/Q3/Q4) and a fake email form. Fixed "← 25" pill to "/", footer link to /43-aviary/guide/.

Hard requirements: prefers-reduced-motion gets a static pre-composed map and stacked cards; canvas pauses when offscreen or the tab is hidden; devicePixelRatio capped at 2; reduced map density and 3 flights on mobile; zero console errors from file://; no horizontal overflow at 390px.

**Asset:** drone product shot generated with Nano Banana 2 via Higgsfield MCP at 2K, palette-locked (white airframe, #FF6B2C cargo pod, pale sky), converted to 1920x1080 WebP with ffmpeg.
