# MASS — Kinetic Type Foundry

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-page site for "MASS", a fictional Swiss type foundry with a loud brutalist attitude. Palette: near-black ink #0A0A0A, acid lime #D8FF3E, warm off-white bone #F2F2EF. Fonts from Google Fonts: Archivo Black for all display type, Space Grotesk for body and UI. Signature technique — scroll-velocity kinetic type: in a single requestAnimationFrame loop, derive scroll velocity (frame-to-frame scrollY delta smoothed with a ~0.1 lerp), clamp it to about ±10, then map it to transforms: the giant stacked hero wordmark gets skewY(velocity × 0.25 degrees) plus a scaleY stretch up to 1.22 with transform-origin left-center; two full-width marquee bands (looping in opposite directions with different durations, e.g. 22s and 30s) receive skewX from the same velocity. Stop writing styles once velocity settles near zero. Add a 120px round cursor blob with mix-blend-mode: difference that lerps toward the pointer and scales to ~2.2× over interactive elements. Layout: full-bleed dark canvas, faint 80px background grid lines, vertical-rl side labels, clamp()-sized type up to 24vw. Sections: hero (wordmark repeated three times — solid, outline via text-stroke, acid), double marquee, manifesto (one huge statement plus three short columns), typeface catalogue as full-width hover rows that invert to acid and skew the name, an interactive glyph specimen grid with a random skew per hover, three license-tier cards, and an acid-lime footer with a viewport-wide wordmark. Copy tone: cocky, terse, industrial — "Set it big or don't set it." Use staggered IntersectionObserver reveals, and under prefers-reduced-motion disable the blob, marquees and all kinetic transforms.
