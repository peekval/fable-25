# STEEP — A Tea House for Slow Hours

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a one-page website for STEEP, a modern tea house and ceremony studio. Palette: celadon #B7C9B1, cream porcelain #F6F1E7, deep tea #4A3728, matcha #7A9B5E. Fonts from Google: Cormorant Infant for headings, Karla for body. The hero is an interactive fluid simulation of tea steeping into clear water: a pool of roughly 3000 particles (half on mobile) advected by an analytic curl-noise velocity field, rendered as short soft strokes onto a canvas that is never cleared — fade it each frame with a translucent cream fill so trails accumulate into ink-like diffusion. Moving the cursor pours tea-brown dye along its path, inheriting stroke momentum; when idle, auto-drips fall every few seconds so the water stays alive. The giant serif title STEEP sits inside the fluid: subtract a blurred glyph mask from the dye each frame so colour flows around and behind the letters. Cap DPR at 2, use a single requestAnimationFrame loop, pause when hidden, and draw one static pre-bloomed swirl for prefers-reduced-motion. Below the hero: a philosophy section with a large italic pull-quote, three tea cards with hand-drawn porcelain-cup SVGs and animated bezier steam wisps, a horizontal ceremony timeline (0:30, 1:00, 2:00) whose gradient fill animates on scroll, and a quiet booking section on deep-tea brown. Mood: calm, precise, ceremonial — generous whitespace, slow easings, nothing loud.
