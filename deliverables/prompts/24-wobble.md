# 24 — WOBBLE · Gummy Candy Brand

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for "WOBBLE", a premium gummy-candy brand that takes joy very seriously. Palette: cream #FFF6E9, bubblegum #FF9EC7, pistachio #A8E6CF, grape #B39DDB, cherry #FF6B81, plum ink #4A2B47 for text and dark sections, soft white #FFFDF9. Fonts: Baloo 2 (600–800) for every headline and button, Quicksand for body. Centerpiece: a hero full of pokeable soft-body 3D gummies — load three.js r147 async from a CDN and render five oversized gummy blobs (spheres, a squashed "bean", one fat gummy-ring torus) in the flavor colors on a transparent canvas behind the headline. Custom ShaderMaterial: the vertex shader does idle breathing plus a faint surface ripple, a traveling wobble ring that decays in space and time from the last poke point, spring-driven squash along the poke axis with a volume-preserving bulge, and velocity stretch; the fragment shader fakes edible translucency with depth-tinted transmission (thick center = deep color, thin rim = light), a tinted fresnel rim whose alpha drops so edges go see-through, dual wet speculars, broad sheen, a backlight glow — and a squinty eyes-and-mouth face that fades in on poked blobs. CPU side: per-blob damped springs for position and squash, click/tap pokes with overshoot wobble, drag-to-fling with settle wobble, cursor brush-pokes, sugar-dust particle puffs (THREE.Points), staggered pop-in intro. Performance: DPR capped at 2, rAF paused when the hero is offscreen (IntersectionObserver) or the tab hides, three lower-poly blobs at ≤768px. Fallback: keep CSS radial-gradient candy divs in the hero for when WebGL or the CDN is missing, and make every CSS keyframe transform/opacity-only — never animate border-radius or SVG d:path(); wave dividers drift a double-width tiled path via translateX instead. Hero headline letters drop and squash on landing with cubic-bezier(.34,1.86,.44,1); buttons read as gummies via stacked inset shadows. Include the dark "flavor lab" console (four gummy-gradient buttons bursting 14 randomized confetti dots, a wobble counter with milestone microcopy at 10/25/50 presses, deadpan status lines), four flavor cards with jiggle ratings, a three-card texture-promise grid, find-us cards, and a footer with wavy-underline links. Copy tone: playful deadpan-legal humor — "Zero sad chews." No sound anywhere. Reduced motion: static gummies rendered once, no jiggle loop, all CSS animations and transitions killed.
