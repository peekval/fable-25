# VEIL & SHADOW — Film-Noir Investigation Bureau (Site 09)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for Veil & Shadow, a fictional private-investigation bureau established 1947, in full film-noir style. Palette: ink-black #0C0C0E, smoke grey #8A8A92, aged paper #E8E6E1, blood red #A11D2A used only for accents and stamps. Fonts (Google): Playfair Display for headlines, Special Elite typewriter face for everything evidentiary — kickers, dossiers, forms. Centerpiece: a raw-WebGL fullscreen fragment shader on a fixed premultiplied-alpha canvas rendering volumetric cigarette smoke over the whole page — two layers of domain-warped fbm (value noise under a rotation matrix; normalize accumulated amplitude so octave count doesn't change density), slowly drifting and curling. The pointer is a desk-lamp beam: a warm scattering core, a working beam, and a wide halo brighten the fog volumetrically from inside, with burn-through at the core so text beneath stays legible; the smoke recoils radially around a second, lagged copy of the light position and re-forms as the lag catches up. Rake venetian-blind stripes diagonally through the haze with an occasional passing-car light sweep along them, add hash-based lamp flicker with rare storm dips and an iris vignette, and compose everything in-shader — 87% shadow falloff outside the beam, unlit smoke silhouetting inside it, luminous fog on top — into premultiplied RGBA so the canvas composites straight over the page. The beam follows mouse and touch; after 3.5 idle seconds it wanders a slow lissajous so it never sits dead. Cap devicePixelRatio at 2, drop to 3 fbm octaves at ≤768px, pause the rAF loop on tab-hide and offscreen via IntersectionObserver, and fall back to a pure-CSS torch — ONE 300vmax fixed element carrying the radial gradients, moved with transform:translate3d only — when WebGL is unavailable or the context is lost; remove both under prefers-reduced-motion. Second signature: a scroll-triggered typewriter — copy lives in the markup for no-JS visitors, then JS stashes and retypes it character by character with randomized 28–74ms delays, longer pauses after periods and dashes, and a blinking blood-red block caret. Add hover-to-reveal redacted spans (background-colored bars that clear on hover or keyboard focus), 3D manila case folders whose covers hinge open via rotateX(−72°) with perspective set on the parent, drifting venetian-blind shadow stripes across the bureau section, and animated feTurbulence film grain over everything. Sections: centered hero with typed tagline, four-service grid with red underline sweeps, three stamped case-file folders, bureau history with stats and a typed field note, numbered contact-drop instructions beside a form whose confirmation types itself, minimal footer. Copy tone: hard-boiled, wry, 1947.
