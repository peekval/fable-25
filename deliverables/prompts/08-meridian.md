# THE MERIDIAN — Art-Deco Grand Hotel (Site 08)

## Full Reproduction Prompt

Build a single-file HTML site for The Meridian, a fictional art-deco grand hotel established 1928, restored 2024. Palette: deep emeralds #0B3D2E / #072A20 / #051F17, gold #D4AF6A with pale highlight #F2DFAE, cream #F4EDDF, and a burgundy bar section #6E1E2B. Fonts (Google): Poiret One for all display type — wide letter-spacing with matching text-indent so centered headings stay optically centered — and Cormorant Garamond for body, italics for asides. Signature one: gold text rendered as a background-clipped linear gradient (bronze → gold → pale gold → bronze) that slowly sheens across headlines. Signature two: self-drawing art-deco SVG ornaments — a hero sunburst of rays, concentric arcs, stepped chevron shoulders and a keystone diamond, every stroke using pathLength=1 with stroke-dasharray/offset and a per-stroke CSS-variable delay so the emblem draws itself in sequence when scrolled into view, fills fading in last. Signature three: a fixed brass elevator panel on the right — round floor buttons (L, M, 5, 41, D) that glow gold when active, italic tooltips, smooth-scroll travel, and scroll-spy via a −42%/−52% rootMargin observer band. Layer a subtle animated feTurbulence film grain and slow parallax conic-gradient ray patterns behind sections. Sections: lobby hero, heritage timeline 1928–2024 on a central gold rail with diamond gems, four room cards with masked 1px gold hairline borders that shimmer on hover, The Gilded Hour bar menu with dotted leaders and prices, a reservations form with underline inputs, footer with a drawn spire mark. Copy tone: wry, impeccable 1928 concierge English. Disable all motion under prefers-reduced-motion.
